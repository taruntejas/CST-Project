Hello this is our Custom Resume Project Synopsys.

Abstract

When a job seeker wants to apply for a job online then generally he /she needs to attach his /her resume with the email.

Online Web profile / Custom Resume creator is a better way to show their resumes to the employers. A job seeker does not need to attach a resume with every email, he/she just have to include the URL of his/her resume and the employer can view the resume online by clicking on the link and can download as well

Introduction

Custom Resume / Web Profile Maker is the Web Application which helps students to get their resume in hand just by filling up a simple form where important credentials need to be filled.

After you fill all the forms we will provide you with the persanalised URL, Which they can share with the employers. One can also download the Profile as a pdf.

Any updation in the resume is easy to make.

Problem Statement

Making Resume is a very hectic work for all the students. And any updation to be made is also defficult.

Eveny time the job seeker has to attach his resume during applying the job. If any changes/ Updation of new things learned can not be done after the resume is submited.
